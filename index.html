My Game Projects

Dark Spells (September 2021)

This is the first game project I participated. I worked on enemy design and engineering, map design, and sound system.

This game is a 2D soul-like game, and with a lore based on Touhou Project. The main character wakes up in the graveyard knowing nothing about herself. Through exploring the dangerous world, she gradually gets to realize the mystery about herself and the approaching disaster.

Since the game producer is still developing this game, I may not be able to provide a playable demo. Hope to see this game soon :)

Run in maze (December 2022)

This game is made for CIS 2022 online program. I made this game with three teammates. I worked as game designer, programmer, and made the input system, AI pathfinding algorithm, maze generation, fog system and vision system.

In this game, player needs to explore the unknown maze, collect the lost treasure, escape from the monster, and find the stairs to the next floor. Since the maze is filled with fog, player needs to use the traps wisely to stay alive.

This game is done with pygame on python, which doesn't have much plug-and-play function. Therefore, I made a simple engine for teammates to use. This made our development fast and smooth.

Melody of Moon (Sept 2024)

This is a serious game made in the Rapid Prototyping course. Made by a group of five, this game aims at showing how we can help people from apathy, which is a mental disorder that makes one lose interests in everything. I came up with the prototype gameplay and accomplished all the scripts in four weeks.

In this game, player is the friend of the moon, who is suffering from apathy. As their friend, player needs to stay with the moon, and guides them to find their own melody from different stars.

Read the Room(Oct 2024)

This is a game made for a gamejam, which theme was "Fight or Flight". I worked with a group of five and made this in three days. I was one of the three engineers. I worked on building the map and different rooms, the combat system, and the entity stats.

In this game, player needs to explore the dungeon, sells the treasures, and eventually meets the quota of the kingdom. However, all the rooms in dungeon is filled with unknown monsters. Try peek inside and not making the monsters aware of you!
